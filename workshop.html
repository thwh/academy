Meditation Workshops

Many of us have become curious about meditation as the evidence of its benefits pile up. As a scientifically-minded engineer, I believe the time has come to liberate the art of meditation from its religious cocoon.

Our classes are a no-nonsense introduction to meditation. We specialize in training a few select groups that benefit from a minimalistic approach:

  • Scientists, engineers, and other skeptics
  • Autistic children
  • Prison populations
  • The physically disabled

As a non-profit, our goal is to offer this service at very low cost to qualifying groups. We will make it work if we believe there is the need.

We offer weekend and daylong workshops onsite. We work primarily in the Bay Area, Reno, and New York City, but almost anywhere in the world is possible. Contact us to discuss.

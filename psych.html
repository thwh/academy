+ Psychoanalysis

What is psychoanalysis? It is talk therapy in its purest state. It is the art of wielding language to change unconscious thinking, which is itself a form of language.

Speech can have the mysterious power to heal. How do we liberate ourselves from the stupidity of words? Only with the magic of words. By developing a neutrality towards our own speech, we learn to accept new, anxious, seemingly impossible thoughts. Behind those impossibilities lies our psychological freedom. Freedom from self-sabotage, from repression, and from the constriction that trauma leaves behind.

"Just say the next thing" as my analyst used to say. There is no one state of being we're searching for. Anxiety, depression, and rage are sometimes healthy. A better sense of humor, a lack of shame, and a general courageousness are the fruits of the path. The "healed" people I know aren't certain and smug -- but grounded, cheerful, and awake.
